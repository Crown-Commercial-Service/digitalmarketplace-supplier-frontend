You, or one of your team, started an application to become a G-Cloud 10 supplier. All contributors on your Digital Marketplace account will now receive email updates. The deadline for completing your application is 5pm BST on Wednesday 23 May 2018.

  • make the supplier declaration

To understand if your services are suitable for G-Cloud 10, read this guidance:
https://www.gov.uk/guidance/g-cloud-suppliers-guide

The G-Cloud 10 application section of your Digital Marketplace account helps you:
  • find all the legal documents and guidance
